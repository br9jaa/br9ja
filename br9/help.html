Identity & Security

How BR9ja handles trust without slowing people down.

Why don't you ask for my BVN during signup? Most Asked

We use a lean onboarding flow. When you fund your wallet for the first time, BR9ja captures the verified sender name from your bank transfer and locks your profile to that legal identity automatically. It is faster, safer, and keeps your BVN private until you need a higher verification tier.

Is it legal to use BR9ja without BVN verification?

BR9ja is designed around low-friction onboarding for low-value wallet activity first. If you need higher-value thresholds or premium services like USD cards, the product can then guide you into a stronger identity upgrade path.

Can I change my profile name later?

No. Once BR9ja captures and locks your verified funding name, that profile name becomes read-only for fraud prevention and identity protection.

Wallet

Funding, refunds, and wallet behavior.

How do I fund my wallet?

Copy your dedicated BR9ja virtual account from the dashboard and transfer from any Nigerian bank app. Successful deposits are credited automatically.

Can I withdraw money back to my bank account?

BR9ja is a fund-and-use platform. Wallet deposits are designed for bills, airtime, data, cards, and other in-platform actions instead of outbound bank withdrawals.

I made a transfer but my wallet isn't credited. What do I do?

Most wallet funding is instant. If a credit delay happens, keep using the same GTBank virtual account details and contact BR9ja support on WhatsApp so the team can review the settlement status.

I accidentally funded too much. Can I get help?

For mistaken high-value deposits, use the Request Refund flow. It opens WhatsApp support with your amount and reference so the team can review the case manually.

Services

Payments, reversals, and premium service clarity.

My electricity or cable payment failed. Will I get a refund?

Failed provider-side transactions are designed to reverse into the BR9ja wallet instead of disappearing. That keeps the correction loop fast and auditable.

Do you support all Nigerian networks for data and airtime?

Yes. BR9ja supports MTN, Airtel, Glo, and 9Mobile across the visible airtime and data surfaces.

How do the USD virtual cards work?

USD cards are positioned as a premium lane for subscriptions, ads, and international checkouts. They can require a stronger verification posture before issuance.

How can I reach a human at BR9ja?

Use the WhatsApp support link for live help or the contact page for structured issue reporting.
