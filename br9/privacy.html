Privacy Policy

We secure payment and identity data because trust is the product.

This policy is designed to align with NDPR expectations in Nigeria and key GDPR principles for transparency, minimization, and user control.

What We Collect

  • Phone number, email, username, and identity details used for onboarding and account recovery.
  • Transaction metadata, service purchase records, and payout activity required for support and fraud checks.
  • Optional passport photos or compliance artifacts submitted for verification and security review.

How We Use It

  • To process utility payments, deliver purchased services, and maintain wallet history.
  • To verify rewards, detect abuse, secure user accounts, and investigate payout issues.
  • To respond to support requests and communicate important account or policy changes.

Security Controls

  • Phone verification is required during signup to reduce fake accounts and payout abuse.
  • Transaction data is protected through server-side validation, rate limits, and authorization checks.
  • Sensitive profile data is restricted to authorized workflows and retained only for legitimate business purposes.

Your Rights

  • You may request correction of inaccurate personal data held by BR9ja.
  • You may contact ops@br9.ng for privacy questions or lawful access requests.
  • Legal and fraud-prevention obligations may require limited retention even after account closure.
